Readability Scores

Flesch-Kincaid

—

Score out of 100 — higher is easier. Weighs sentence length and syllable count.

Gunning Fog

—

Grade level estimate based on sentence length and complex (3+ syllable) words.

SMOG Index

—

Grade level estimate focused on polysyllabic words. Favoured in healthcare comms.

Email target zone

Aim for a Flesch-Kincaid score of 60 or above and a Gunning Fog / SMOG grade of 8 or below — roughly a comfortable 8th-grade reading level for most audiences.

These benchmarks suit broad consumer audiences. For professional, technical, or B2B readers, higher complexity is often appropriate — treat the scores as a starting point, not a rule.
