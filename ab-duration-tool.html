A/B Test Duration Calculator

In Holistic Testing, duration is planned before a test begins — not guessed at, and not cut short when early numbers look promising. Start with your hypothesis, then use this calculator to find out how long your test genuinely needs to run.

Your hypothesis doesn't affect the calculation — but writing it before you look at the numbers is the discipline that makes results interpretable and reusable.

Choose the metric that maps directly to your test objective — not just the easiest one to measure. Opens are a weak proxy for most goals; clicks, conversions, or revenue per email are usually more meaningful.

Holistic Testing uses a true 50/50 split. Avoid 10/10/80 structures — they optimise for speed, not reliability.

Reaching duration is the start, not the finish

When your test completes, a statistically significant result is a validated signal — not a confirmed conclusion. Holistic Testing treats one significant result as the trigger for validation: repeat the finding across a different segment or send occasion before rolling out. This is how marginal gains compound reliably over time.
