We-We Calculator

Is your copy talking about you, or to your reader? Paste your email below and see the brand vs. customer balance at a glance.

How it works: The calculator counts brand-centric pronouns (we, us, our, I, me, my) and customer-centric pronouns (you, your), then shows you the split. Note that first-person I is included in the brand count — so naturally conversational copy will score more brand-focused than it really is. Treat the result as a directional guide, not a precise measure.
